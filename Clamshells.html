Clamshells

Protect and present your product.

Clamshells are an economical means of packaging your product for shipping, storage and display in the retail market. They can be kept closed with snaps for ease of opening and re-use or welded shut for security and tamper evidence. Printed inserts can be enclosed to provide consumers with product information, display eye catching colored art work and gain brand recognition.
